Job Title: DevOps / Cloud Engineer

Company: CloudFirst Solutions
Location: Remote
Type: Full-Time

About the Role:
We are seeking a DevOps Engineer to build and maintain our cloud infrastructure,
CI/CD pipelines, and deployment automation. You will work at the intersection of
development and operations to ensure reliable, scalable systems.

Required Skills:
- Cloud Platforms: AWS (EC2, ECS, EKS, Lambda, S3, RDS) or GCP or Azure
- Containerization: Docker, Kubernetes (EKS/GKE/AKS)
- CI/CD: GitHub Actions, Jenkins, ArgoCD, or GitLab CI
- Infrastructure as Code: Terraform, CloudFormation, or Pulumi
- Scripting: Bash, Python
- Linux Administration (Ubuntu, CentOS)
- Networking: VPC, load balancers, DNS, SSL/TLS
- Version Control: Git

Preferred / Bonus Skills:
- Service Mesh: Istio or Linkerd
- Monitoring & Observability: Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack, DataDog
- Secret Management: HashiCorp Vault, AWS Secrets Manager
- Helm charts for Kubernetes
- Ansible or Chef for configuration management
- Cost optimization and FinOps practices

Experience Requirements:
- 3+ years of DevOps or SRE experience
- Experience managing production Kubernetes clusters
- Incident response and on-call experience

Responsibilities:
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage cloud infrastructure (multi-region)
- Implement security best practices and compliance
- Automate deployment and scaling processes
- Monitor system health and respond to incidents
- Collaborate with development teams on architecture decisions
