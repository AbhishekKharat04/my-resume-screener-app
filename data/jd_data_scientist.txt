Job Title: Senior Data Scientist

Company: Analytics Corp
Location: Remote / Hybrid
Type: Full-Time

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our data analytics team.
You will develop predictive models, conduct advanced statistical analysis,
and translate complex data insights into actionable business recommendations.

Required Skills:
- Python (advanced proficiency)
- Statistical Analysis: hypothesis testing, regression, Bayesian methods
- Machine Learning: scikit-learn, XGBoost, random forests, neural networks
- Deep Learning: TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Data Visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, Tableau
- SQL (advanced queries, window functions, CTEs)
- Data Wrangling: Pandas, NumPy, data cleaning
- Jupyter Notebooks / Lab

Preferred / Bonus Skills:
- NLP: text classification, sentiment analysis, transformers
- Big Data: Spark, Hadoop, or Databricks
- Cloud: AWS (Redshift, S3) or GCP (BigQuery)
- A/B Testing and experiment design
- R programming
- Docker for reproducible environments

Experience Requirements:
- 3+ years of professional data science experience
- Experience presenting insights to non-technical stakeholders
- Published research or Kaggle competition experience is a plus

Responsibilities:
- Build predictive and prescriptive models
- Design and analyze A/B tests
- Create dashboards and automated reports
- Collaborate with engineering to deploy models
- Mentor junior data scientists
