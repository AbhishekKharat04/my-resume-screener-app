Job Title: Product Manager

Company: InnovateTech
Location: Remote / Hybrid
Type: Full-Time

About the Role:
We are seeking a Product Manager to drive the strategy and execution of our
core platform products. You will work cross-functionally with engineering,
design, and business teams to deliver products that delight users.

Required Skills:
- Product Strategy: roadmap planning, prioritization frameworks (RICE, MoSCoW)
- User Research: user interviews, surveys, usability testing
- Data Analysis: SQL, Excel/Google Sheets, product analytics
- Analytics Tools: Mixpanel, Amplitude, Google Analytics, or Heap
- Agile/Scrum: sprint planning, backlog management, JIRA
- Wireframing: Figma, Sketch, or Balsamiq
- Communication: PRDs, user stories, stakeholder presentations
- A/B Testing: experiment design and analysis

Preferred / Bonus Skills:
- Technical Background: basic coding, API understanding
- Growth Product Management experience
- B2B SaaS or marketplace experience
- Financial modeling and business case development
- Design thinking and Jobs-to-be-Done framework
- SQL proficiency for self-serve analytics
- Experience with AI/ML products

Experience Requirements:
- 3+ years of product management experience
- Track record of shipping products 0-to-1 or scaling existing products
- Experience managing cross-functional teams of 5+ people

Responsibilities:
- Define product vision, strategy, and roadmap
- Prioritize features based on user needs and business impact
- Write detailed PRDs and user stories
- Collaborate with engineering and design sprints
- Analyze product metrics and user feedback
- Present product updates to leadership
- Conduct competitive analysis and market research
